Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 6, 2025

FundIQSG ("we", "our", or "us") is a local, offline-only accounting app designed to help you manage your personal finances securely on your own device. Your privacy is very important to us.

1. No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal or financial data. All information you enter into FundIQSG remains on your device and is never shared with us or any third parties.

2. No Internet Connection Required

FundIQSG operates fully offline. The app does not require an internet connection to function, and it does not communicate with any servers or external services.

3. Data Security

Because your data is stored locally on your device, you are solely responsible for backing it up and keeping your device secure. We recommend enabling device-level security features such as screen lock or encryption.

4. No Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics tools, advertising networks, or external libraries that would collect data from your device.

5. Children's Privacy

FundIQSG is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Although this app does not collect data, we may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in the app or applicable laws. Any updates will be posted on this page with the revised date.
